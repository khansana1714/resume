Our Products

Split lock washer

A split lock washer is used to prevent nuts and bolts from loosening due to vibration and torque by providing a spring-like action that grips the fastener, maintaining a tight connection.

External & Internal Tooth Washer

External & Internal Tooth Washer have teeth that extend outward & Inward, gripping the material being fastened. They provide greater holding power and are suitable for larger fasteners or applications where a strong grip is needed.

Fender washer

A Fender washer is used to distribute the load of a bolt or screw over a larger area, providing a more secure hold and preventing the fastener from pulling through the material. It is commonly used in automotive, plumbing, and sheet metal applications.

Finishing washer

A Finishing washer is used to create a clean, professional appearance for exposed fasteners, typically in decorative or visible applications. It provides a smooth, finished look while helping to distribute the load of the fastener.

square washer

A square washer is used to distribute the load of a bolt or screw over a larger area, especially in wooden constructions or when using a square-headed bolt. It provides better resistance to rotation and can be used to prevent the fastener from pulling through the material.

An ogee washer

An ogee washer, also known as a curved or decorative washer, is used to provide an aesthetically pleasing finish while distributing the load of the fastener. It is commonly used in furniture, railing, and other applications where appearance is important.

plain washer

A plain washer is used to distribute the load of a bolt or screw, reducing stress on the material and preventing damage. It also helps to prevent the fastener from loosening and protects the surface from abrasion

rectangle washer

A rectangle washer is used to distribute the load of a bolt or screw over a larger area, particularly in applications where there is a need to cover elongated slots or provide additional support in specific directions. It offers increased surface contact and stability, reducing the risk of pull-through and material deformation.
